<p><p>Electroless copper plating additives market is divided into three types: brightening agents, surfactants, and others. Brightening agents are substances added to the plating solution to enhance the appearance and shine of the copper coating. Surfactants, on the other hand, are additives that reduce surface tension to ensure a uniform spread of the plating solution and improve the adhesion of the copper layer. The category "others" includes additives with different functions, such as leveling agents, stabilizers, and accelerators, which contribute to the effectiveness and quality of the electroless copper plating process.</p></p>
