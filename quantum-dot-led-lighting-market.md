<p><strong>The Quantum Dot LED Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Remote QD LED lights</li><li>On-Chip QD LED lights</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quantum Dot LED lighting market can be categorized into three types: remote QD LED lights, on-chip QD LED lights, and others. Remote QD LED lights are composed of separate quantum dot packages that are connected to conventional LEDs, allowing for an enhanced color range. On-chip QD LED lights, on the other hand, integrate the quantum dots directly onto the LED chip, resulting in improved energy efficiency and color performance. The "others" category includes emerging technologies and innovations that do not fall into the remote or on-chip QD LED lights types.</p></p>
