<p><p>The Industrial GigE Cameras market consists of two main types: Area Scan Cameras and Line Scan Cameras. Area Scan Cameras capture an image of a specific area, similar to a regular digital camera. They are suitable for inspection and quality control applications. On the other hand, Line Scan Cameras capture images line by line, making them ideal for continuous inspection of moving objects. Both types offer high-resolution image quality and are commonly used in industrial settings for various vision-based applications such as machine vision, robotics, and automation.</p></p>
