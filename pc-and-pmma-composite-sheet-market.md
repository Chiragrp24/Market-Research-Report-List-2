<p><strong>The PC and PMMA Composite Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-layer</li><li>Three-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PC and PMMA composite sheet market types refer to the construction of a composite sheet using two or three layers of different materials. In a two-layer sheet, two distinct layers of PC and PMMA are fused together, combining their individual properties. This provides enhanced strength, impact resistance, and transparency in the sheet. On the other hand, a three-layer sheet consists of a PC layer sandwiched between two PMMA layers, offering even higher strength, impact resistance, and improved UV protection. Different layer configurations cater to varying market demands and applications.</p></p>
