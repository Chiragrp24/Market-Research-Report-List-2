<p><p>Photovoltaic DC isolators are used in solar energy systems to safely disconnect the direct current (DC) power generated by the photovoltaic panels. The market for these isolators can be categorized into two types: 2 Pole and 4 Pole. </p><p>A 2 Pole isolator refers to a device that has two poles or two contact points. It is typically used when there is only one DC positive and one DC negative conductor that needs to be disconnected. </p><p>On the other hand, a 4 Pole isolator has four poles or four contact points. It is used when there are two DC positive and two DC negative conductors that need to be disconnected, such as in systems with multiple strings of photovoltaic panels.</p></p>
