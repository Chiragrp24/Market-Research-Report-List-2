<p><p>Polyvinyl films are available in various types, namely Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE), Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE), High Density Polyethylene (HDPE), and Crosslinked Polyethylene. LDPE films are flexible, transparent, and commonly used for packaging, while MDPE films have improved strength and puncture resistance. HDPE films offer higher rigidity and moisture barrier properties, making them suitable for agricultural and industrial applications. Crosslinked Polyethylene films possess enhanced durability and heat resistance, and are widely used in wire and cable insulation, automotive components, and medical industry.</p></p>
