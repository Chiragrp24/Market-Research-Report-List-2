<p><p>A Silicon Controlled Rectifier (SCR) is a solid-state device that acts as a controllable switch for high power rectification applications. It is a four-layer PNPN semiconductor device that operates as a switch, conducting current in the forward direction once it is triggered and allowing current flow even after the triggering signal is removed. SCR's unique characteristic of latching until the forward current drops below a certain threshold makes it suitable for applications such as motor speed control, power supplies, lighting control, and welding equipment.</p><p>The future outlook of the SCR market is highly promising.
