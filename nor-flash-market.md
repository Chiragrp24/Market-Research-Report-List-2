<p><strong>The NOR Flash Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Serial NOR Flash</li><li>Parallel NOR Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NOR Flash market consists of two main types: Serial NOR Flash and Parallel NOR Flash. Serial NOR Flash is known for its high performance and small form factor, making it ideal for applications like automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial devices. It offers fast read and write speeds, allowing for quick execution of code. On the other hand, Parallel NOR Flash is more commonly used in legacy systems where higher bus widths are necessary. It provides greater bandwidth but requires more pins and is often found in applications like gaming, networking, and telecommunication.</p></p>
