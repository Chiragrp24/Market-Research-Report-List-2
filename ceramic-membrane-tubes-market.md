SiC membrane tubes, on the other hand, utilize silicon carbide, known for its exceptional mechanical strength and thermal conductivity. Both types of ceramic membrane tubes are widely used in various industries, including water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food and beverage, due to their superior filtration capabilities and durability.</p></p>
