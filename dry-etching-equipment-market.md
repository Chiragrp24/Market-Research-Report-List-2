<p><p>Dry etching equipment is used in the semiconductor industry to remove material from a substrate during the manufacturing process. The market types for dry etching equipment include Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP), Capacitive Coupled Plasma (CCP), Reactive Ion Etching (RIE), Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE), and Others. ICP uses electromagnetic fields to generate plasma, while CCP uses a capacitive coupling mechanism. RIE utilizes reactive gases to effectively etch materials, and DRIE is a specialized form of RIE used for deep and precise etching. The "Others" category includes alternative dry etching techniques.</p></p>
