<p><p>The standard headband style earmuffs refer to the traditional type that has a plastic or metal band that goes over the top of the head and cushions that cover the ears. They are widely available and commonly used for hearing protection in various industries and personal settings. On the other hand, wrap-around earmuffs have a different design, with a band that wraps around the back of the head and cushions that enclose the ears completely. This type provides a more secure fit and is suitable for environments with higher noise levels or increased movement.</p></p>
