<p><p>Textile water repellent refers to a type of finishing treatment applied to textiles to make them resistant to water penetration. This treatment creates a barrier on the surface of the fabric, causing water droplets to roll off rather than being absorbed. It helps to prevent moisture from seeping through the textile and keeps it dry in wet conditions. Textile water repellents are commonly used in outdoor apparel, sportswear, and protective gear, where maintaining the fabric's breathable properties while repelling water is crucial.</p><p>The future outlook for the textile water repellent market is promising due to the increasing demand for water-repellent fabrics across various industries.
