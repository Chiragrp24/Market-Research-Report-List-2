<p><p>Excimer laser gas is used as the active medium in excimer lasers, which emit high-energy ultraviolet light. The excimer laser gas market comprises different types of gases, including balance gas Ne (neon) and balance gas He (helium). Balance gas Ne is added to achieve the desired operating pressure and stabilize the discharge, resulting in improved laser efficiency. On the other hand, balance gas He reduces the heat load on the laser tube and helps maintain a stable discharge at higher operating powers. Both gases play crucial roles in optimizing excimer laser performance.</p></p>
