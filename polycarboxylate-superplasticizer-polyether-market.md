TPEG stands for polyethylene glycol modified by polyether, MPEG refers to polyethylene glycol modified by methoxy, HPEG stands for hydroxyethyl modified by polyether, APEG refers to amino polyether, and the "others" category includes various other types of polyether chains. These different types offer different performance characteristics and are selected based on the specific requirements of the construction project.</p></p>
