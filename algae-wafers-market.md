<p><p>Algae wafers are a popular fish food option, and they are available in two different market types: sinking particles and non-sinking particles. Sinking particles refer to wafers that quickly sink to the bottom of the aquarium, allowing bottom-dwelling fish to easily consume them. On the other hand, non-sinking particles are designed to float in the water, ensuring that fish from all levels of the aquarium can access the food. Both market types cater to the dietary needs of different fish species and provide a convenient and nutritious feeding option for aquarium owners.</p></p>
