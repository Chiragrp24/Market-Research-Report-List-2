<p><p>Nitrile cellular rubber is available in two main market types: closed cell rubber and semi-closed cell rubber. Closed cell rubber has completely sealed cells, which prevent the passage of gases or liquids, making it suitable for applications requiring insulation or waterproofing. On the other hand, semi-closed cell rubber has partially sealed cells, allowing some gases or liquids to pass through, making it ideal for applications that require breathability or cushioning. Both market types offer unique properties and are used in various industries, such as automotive, construction, and aerospace.</p></p>
