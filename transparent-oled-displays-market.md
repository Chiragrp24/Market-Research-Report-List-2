<p><p>Transparent OLED Displays are a type of display technology that allows users to see through the screen while displaying vibrant images and videos. They are available in two market types - AMOLED (Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode) and PMOLED (Passive-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode). </p><p>AMOLED displays utilize a backplane of switches to control pixels individually, resulting in higher resolution and faster response time. On the other hand, PMOLED displays use a simpler matrix of controls, making them more cost-effective but with lower resolution and slower response time. Both display types are used in various applications like smartphones, wearables, and automotive displays.</p></p>
