<p><ul><li>Front</li><li>Rear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle derailleur market can be categorized into two types: front and rear. The front derailleur is responsible for shifting the chain between the chainrings located at the front crankset, allowing the rider to choose between different gear ratios. On the other hand, the rear derailleur shifts the chain between the different cogs on the rear cassette, enabling the selection of various gear combinations. Both front and rear derailleurs play a crucial role in controlling the movement of the chain, ensuring smooth gear changes, and thus providing an efficient cycling experience.</p></p>
