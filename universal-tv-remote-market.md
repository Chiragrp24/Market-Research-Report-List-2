<p><p>There are two main types of universal TV remote market: infrared remote and Bluetooth remote. Infrared remotes use infrared signals to transmit commands to the TV, requiring a clear line of sight between the remote and the TV for them to work. On the other hand, Bluetooth remotes utilize Bluetooth technology to connect with the TV, enabling operation even without direct line of sight. Both types of remotes offer convenience and compatibility with multiple TV brands, but differ in their method of signal transmission.</p></p>
