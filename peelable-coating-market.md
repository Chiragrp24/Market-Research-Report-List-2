<p><p>Peelable coating is a type of protective layer that can be easily removed without leaving any residue behind. It is used in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and packaging. There are three main types of peelable coatings: solvent-based, water-based, and hot-melt. Solvent-based coatings are made using organic solvents and offer excellent adhesion and resistance. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly and provide good peelability. Hot-melt coatings are applied in a molten state and solidify upon cooling, offering strong adhesion and easy removal. Each type has its own benefits and is used based on specific requirements.</p></p>
