<p><p>The Lower Carbon Cements market encompasses various types of cement that have reduced carbon emissions compared to traditional Portland cement. Fly Ash based Cement is made by combining fly ash, a waste product of coal burning, with cement. Slag based Cement is produced by blending ground granulated blast furnace slag with cement. Geopolymer Cement is made from industrial by-products or natural materials using an alkaline solution without the use of limestone. Other markets in this industry include calcium aluminate cements and limestone calcined clay cements. All these alternatives aim to provide sustainable and environmentally friendly options for construction.</p></p>
