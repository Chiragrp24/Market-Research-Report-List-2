<p><p>The Passive LC Filter Market is divided into various types. Low Pass Filters enable low-frequency signals while blocking high-frequency ones. High Pass Filters do the opposite, allowing high-frequency signals and blocking low-frequency ones. Band Pass Filters allow signals within a specific range, blocking those outside that range. Other filter types include Band Stop Filters, which block signals within a specific range, and All Pass Filters, which let all signals pass through but alter their phase. These different filter types cater to diverse applications and signal requirements.</p></p>
