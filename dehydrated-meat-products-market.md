<p><p>The dehydrated meat products market offers various types of processing methods to remove moisture from meats. Sun-dried involves air drying under natural sunlight, while hot air drying uses heated air to remove moisture. Vacuum drying is done in a low-pressure environment to accelerate the process. Spray drying uses a high-velocity hot air stream to atomize and dry the meat. Lastly, freeze drying involves freezing the meat first, then removing the ice through sublimation under vacuum. Each method offers unique benefits and characteristics for dehydrated meat products.</p></p>
