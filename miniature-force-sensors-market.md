<p><p>Miniature force sensors are devices used to measure and detect the force or pressure applied on objects. Piezoresistive force sensors work by measuring the change in electrical resistance caused by applied force. Ultrasonic force sensors use sound waves to measure the force exerted on an object. Capacitive force sensors measure changes in capacitance when a force is applied. Optical force sensors use light to measure force. Magnetic force sensors detect force using magnetic fields. Other types of miniature force sensors include strain gauge force sensors and load cell force sensors. These sensors find applications in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and healthcare.</p></p>
