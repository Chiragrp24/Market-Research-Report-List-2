<p><p>E beam crucibles are used in electron beam evaporation processes to hold and vaporize the materials to be deposited on a substrate. These crucibles come in different shapes, with the two main types being round and cone-shaped. Round crucibles have a cylindrical shape, providing a larger surface area for material vaporization. Cone crucibles have a tapering shape, allowing for more efficient vapor deposition. The choice between round and cone crucibles depends on the specific application and the material being evaporated.</p></p>
