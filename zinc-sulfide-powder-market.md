<p><p>Zinc sulfide powder is a compound formed by the chemical reaction between zinc and sulfur. It is a white or yellowish powder with a wide application in various industries. Zinc sulfide powder is also utilized in optical devices, pigments, and in the production of various chemicals.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the global zinc sulfide powder market is expected to witness robust growth during the forecast period.
