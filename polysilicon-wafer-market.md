<p><p>The polysilicon wafer market consists of two main types: P type and N type. P type polysilicon wafers are created by introducing boron atoms into the silicon crystal structure, resulting in a positive charge. These wafers are commonly used in the manufacturing of solar cells. On the other hand, N type polysilicon wafers are created by adding phosphorus atoms, which gives them a negative charge. These wafers are widely used in the production of integrated circuits and other electronic devices. Both P type and N type polysilicon wafers have their unique applications in different industries.</p></p>
