<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Micro Channel Aluminum Flat Tube is commonly used in heat exchangers and radiators due to its high thermal conductivity. There are two main types: parallel flow flat tube and single hole flat tube. </p><p>Parallel flow flat tube refers to a design where multiple refrigerant tubes run parallel to each other in a flat configuration. This design allows for efficient heat transfer, as it provides a larger surface area for cooling.</p><p>On the other hand, single hole flat tube consists of a single tube with holes punched in it to improve heat transfer. This design provides better heat dissipation and allows for more compact and lightweight heat exchangers.</p></p>
