<p><p>The refrigerant gas sensor market encompasses various types of sensors, including electrochemical, semiconductor, pellistor, and infrared sensors. Electrochemical sensors use chemical reactions to detect and measure refrigerant gases, semiconductor sensors utilize the conductivity changes caused by gas absorption, pellistor sensors rely on catalytic combustion to detect gases, and infrared sensors detect gases by measuring the absorption of infrared energy. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, offering different levels of accuracy, response time, and sensitivity for monitoring refrigerant gas leaks in different applications.</p></p>
