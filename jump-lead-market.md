<p><p>Jump leads, also known as jumper cables or booster cables, are electrical cables used to connect the batteries of two vehicles. They are primarily used to jump-start a vehicle when its battery has been depleted or has failed. Jump leads consist of a pair of insulated cables with alligator clips at each end, which are connected to the positive and negative terminals of the batteries.</p><p>The current outlook of the jump lead market is positive, with a steady growth rate expected in the coming years. The increasing number of vehicle breakdowns and the rising demand for emergency roadside assistance services are driving the demand for jump leads. Additionally, the growing number of vehicles with electrical systems and the need for jump leads in industrial and commercial sectors are also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology have led to the development of smart jump leads, which incorporate safety mechanisms and can be connected to smartphones for remote monitoring and control.
