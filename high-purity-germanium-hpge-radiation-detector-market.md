<p><p>The High Purity Germanium (HPGe) radiation detector market can be classified into three types: P-Type Coaxial, P-Type Semi-Planar, and N-Type Coaxial. </p><p>P-Type Coaxial detectors have a coaxial electrode arrangement that allows high resolution and efficiency, making them suitable for gamma spectroscopy applications. </p><p>P-Type Semi-Planar detectors have a partially optimized electrode design, offering a balance between resolution and efficiency. They are commonly used in environmental monitoring and nuclear medicine. </p><p>N-Type Coaxial detectors utilize a different electrode configuration, which provides enhanced resolution at lower energy ranges, making them valuable for low-level neutron detection and special nuclear material security applications.</p></p>
