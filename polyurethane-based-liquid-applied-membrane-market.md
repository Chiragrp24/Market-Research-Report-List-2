<p><p>The polyurethane-based liquid-applied membrane market is categorized into two types: one component and two component. One component membranes are pre-mixed and ready to use, eliminating the need for complex mixing procedures. They offer convenience and ease of application. On the other hand, two component membranes require the mixing of two separate components before application. This allows for more precise formulation and customization based on specific project requirements. Both types of membranes provide excellent waterproofing and are commonly used in construction and roofing applications.</p></p>
