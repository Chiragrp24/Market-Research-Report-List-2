<p><strong>The Platinum RTD Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound RTD</li><li>Thin Film RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Platinum RTD Sensors market is segmented into two main types: Wire-Wound RTD and Thin Film RTD. Wire-Wound RTD sensors are made by winding a platinum wire around a ceramic or glass core, which provides greater resistance to temperature changes. Thin Film RTD sensors, on the other hand, are manufactured by depositing a thin layer of platinum on a ceramic substrate, resulting in a more compact and sensitive sensor. Both types offer accurate temperature measurement and find applications in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and healthcare.</p></p>
