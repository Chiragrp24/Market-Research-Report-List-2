<p><p>The Aqueous Polyurethane Resin market consists of two main types: Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion and Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion. Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion is a water-based resin that forms a dispersion of the resin particles in water. It is commonly used for coating applications due to its excellent adhesion and durability properties. Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion, on the other hand, is a water-based mixture of polyurethane particles and water, which can be further diluted with water for applications such as paints and coatings. Both types offer environmental advantages and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
