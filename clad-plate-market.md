<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Clad plate market types include explosion bonded and roll bonded clad plates. Explosion bonded clad plates are created by bonding two metal layers with the help of explosive energy, resulting in a strong metallurgical bond. These plates are commonly used in industries requiring corrosion resistance and wear resistance. On the other hand, roll bonded clad plates involve the application of heat and pressure to bond two or more metal layers together. These plates are used in various industries, such as oil and gas, chemical, and automotive, due to their durability and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
