<p><p>Calcium hydroxide, also known as slaked lime or hydrated lime, is a white, inorganic compound with the chemical formula Ca(OH)2. It is derived from limestone or calcium oxide through a process called slaking, where the calcium oxide is mixed with water, resulting in the formation of calcium hydroxide.</p><p>The calcium hydroxide market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. The compound finds applications in various sectors such as construction, agriculture, water treatment, and chemical industries due to its unique properties. In the construction industry, it is used as a component in mortars, concrete, and plasters. In the agriculture sector, it is utilized as a soil pH regulator and a source of calcium for plants. Moreover, it is also employed in the treatment of drinking water and wastewater to adjust pH levels and remove impurities.</p><p>One of the major drivers for the growth of the calcium hydroxide market is the increasing demand for construction materials in developing economies. The rapid urbanization and infrastructure development projects are fueling the demand for calcium hydroxide-based materials. Additionally, the growing awareness about water treatment and the need for clean water is boosting the adoption of calcium hydroxide for purification purposes.</p><p>However, there are certain challenges faced by the market that may hinder its growth. These challenges include the availability of alternatives such as sodium hydroxide and potassium hydroxide, which can limit the market growth to some extent.</p><p>Overall, with the growing construction and water treatment sectors, along with increasing awareness about the benefits of calcium hydroxide, the market is expected to witness steady growth. The forecast period predicts a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 5.7% for the calcium hydroxide market.</p></p>
