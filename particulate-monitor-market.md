<p><p>The particulate monitor market consists of two main types: fixed particulate monitors and portable particulate monitors. Fixed particulate monitors are designed to be permanently installed in a specific location and continuously measure the levels of particulate matter in the air. They are commonly used in industrial settings or in areas with known pollution sources. On the other hand, portable particulate monitors are designed to be portable and easily transported to different locations. They are typically used for short-term monitoring, such as for research purposes or to assess air quality in different areas.</p></p>
