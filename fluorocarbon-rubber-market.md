There are various types of FKM in the market, including Vinylidene Fluoride (VF2) copolymers (VD), Hydrogenated Fluorocarbon Polymers (HFP), Tetrafluoroethylene (TFE) copolymers, Perfluoromethylvinylether (PMVE), and others. VD is known for its excellent resistance to oil and solvents, while HFP offers improved resistance to heat and bases. TFE copolymers provide superior resistance to chemicals and high temperatures, whereas PMVE exhibits low-temperature flexibility and excellent compression set resistance.</p></p>
