<p><strong>The RF Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2.4 GHz</li><li>Sub-GHz</li><li>2.4 GHz / 5 GHz</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF (Radio Frequency) modules are communication devices that operate in the radio frequency range. The market is divided into three types: 2.4 GHz, Sub-GHz, and 2.4 GHz/5 GHz. </p><p>2.4 GHz modules use the 2.4 GHz frequency band, which is crowded due to various wireless devices like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and Zigbee. </p><p>Sub-GHz modules operate below 1 GHz, providing longer range and better penetration through obstacles.</p><p>2.4 GHz/5 GHz modules offer dual-band functionality, allowing communication in both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequency bands, providing versatility and reduced interference. These modules are commonly used in Wi-Fi routers and other wireless communication devices.</p></p>
