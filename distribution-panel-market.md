<p><p>The distribution panel market includes two main types: low voltage and medium voltage. Low voltage distribution panels are used for power distribution in residential and commercial buildings with voltages below 1kV. They serve as a central hub for connecting power from the main supply to various electrical circuits within the premises. On the other hand, medium voltage distribution panels are designed for voltages between 1kV and 33kV, primarily used in industrial settings to distribute power from the main source to large machinery and equipment. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient electricity distribution.</p></p>
