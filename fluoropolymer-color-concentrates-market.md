<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluoropolymer color concentrates are available in two main types: liquid and solid. Liquid color concentrates are formulated as a liquid dispersion of fluoropolymer resins and pigment powders. They are typically used for coloring fluoropolymer resins during processing. On the other hand, solid color concentrates are produced as solid pellets or chips. They are added to the fluoropolymer resin in the melt phase and disperse evenly during processing. Both liquid and solid color concentrates offer ease of use, improved color consistency, and enhanced material properties in various applications.</p></p>
