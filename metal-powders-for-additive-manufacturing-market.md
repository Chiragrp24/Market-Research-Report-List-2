<p><p>The metal powders for additive manufacturing market consists of various types, including powder bed, blown powder, and others. </p><p>Powder bed is a popular method where a thin layer of metal powder is spread on a build plate, and a laser or electron beam selectively melts the powder to form each layer of the 3D printed object. </p><p>In the blown powder process, metal powder is fed into a nozzle where it is melted, atomized, and propelled onto the build platform using gas or kinetic energy. This method allows for greater flexibility in materials and can produce larger parts. </p><p>Other types may include methods such as binder jetting or directed energy deposition, which offer unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
