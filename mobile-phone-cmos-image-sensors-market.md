<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The mobile phone CMOS image sensor market consists of two major types: stacked CMOS and back-illuminated CMOS. Stacked CMOS sensors have separate layers for capturing light and processing signals, enabling higher pixel count and better image quality. Back-illuminated CMOS sensors, on the other hand, have a design that maximizes light capture by placing the photodiode layer at the top, resulting in improved low-light performance and higher sensitivity. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in enhancing the image quality and capabilities of mobile phone cameras.</p></p>
