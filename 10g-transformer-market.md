<p><p>The 10G transformer market comprises of two main types: PoE (Power over Ethernet) and Non-PoE transformers. PoE transformers enable the transmission of both data and power over Ethernet cables, providing a convenient solution for devices such as IP cameras and wireless access points. Non-PoE transformers, on the other hand, are used solely for data transmission purposes and do not deliver power. Both market segments cater to different needs, with PoE transformers offering added functionality and flexibility for power-dependent network devices.</p></p>
