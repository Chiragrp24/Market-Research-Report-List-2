<p><strong>The Manioc Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sweet Manioc Flour</li><li>Bitter Manioc Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Manioc flour comes in two main market types: sweet manioc flour and bitter manioc flour. Sweet manioc flour is made from the sweet variety of the manioc plant, which is naturally low in cyanide content. It is commonly used in baking, cooking, and as a thickening agent. Bitter manioc flour, on the other hand, is derived from the bitter variety of the manioc plant, which contains higher levels of cyanide. This flour is mainly utilized for industrial purposes, such as the production of adhesive materials or livestock feed.</p></p>
