<p><p>The Middle Voltage Circuit Breakers Market includes three main types: Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breaker (ACB). MCBs are commonly used in homes and small commercial buildings, providing protection against short circuits and overloads. MCCBs are larger and used in industrial applications, offering higher current ratings and advanced protection features. ACBs are designed for high voltage applications and are commonly found in power distribution systems, protecting against overloads and faults. Each type serves specific purposes depending on the scale and requirements of the electrical system.</p></p>
