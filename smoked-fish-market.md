<p><p>The smoked fish market is typically divided into two main types: hot smoked fish and cold smoked fish. Hot smoking involves cooking the fish at a higher temperature, resulting in a fully cooked and flaky texture with a smoky flavor. Cold smoking, on the other hand, is a process where the fish is smoked at a lower temperature without cooking it completely. This method imparts a milder smoky flavor and leaves the fish with a firmer texture. Both hot and cold smoked fish varieties are popular choices among seafood enthusiasts.</p></p>
