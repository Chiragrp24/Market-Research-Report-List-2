<p><p>The Marine Epoxy Putty market consists of two main types: Water-based Epoxy Putty and Oil-based Epoxy Putty. Water-based Epoxy Putty is made by combining epoxy resin with water as the solvent. It offers advantages like low odor, easy cleanup, and environmental friendliness. On the other hand, Oil-based Epoxy Putty is made using oil as the solvent, providing better adhesion and durability. Although it may have a stronger odor and require extra precautions during application, it is known for its excellent performance in harsh marine environments.</p></p>
