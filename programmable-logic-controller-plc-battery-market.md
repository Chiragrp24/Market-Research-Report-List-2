<p><p>The programmable logic controller (PLC) battery market finds application in the areas of discrete I/O and analog I/O. Discrete I/O refers to digital signals that represent binary states, such as on/off or open/close. PLC batteries are essential in maintaining continuous power supply to manage these digital signals and ensure consistent operation of devices such as sensors, switches, and actuators. Analog I/O involves continuous signals that convey varying values, like temperature or pressure. PLC batteries play a vital role in providing uninterrupted power to control and monitor these analog signals accurately within industrial automation systems.</p></p>
