<p><p>Low Temperature Cofired Ceramics (LTCC) refers to a type of multilayer ceramic packaging technology that is widely used in the electronic industry. LTCC materials are widely used in these devices for their excellent electrical and thermal properties, as well as their ability to provide miniaturized and lightweight packaging solutions.</p><p>Another factor driving the market growth is the rising demand for automotive electronics.
