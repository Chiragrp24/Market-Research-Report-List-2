<p><p>The force sensor market consists of various types of sensors used to measure force. Capacitive force sensors work by measuring the change in capacitance when a force is applied. Piezo-resistive force sensors use strain gauges to measure the resistance changes caused by force. Piezo-electric force sensors generate an electric charge in response to applied force. Magneto-elastic force sensors work by measuring changes in magnetic field caused by force. Each type of sensor has its own advantages and applications, catering to different industries and measurement requirements.</p></p>
