<p><p>The embedded biometric modules market consists of various types of identification technologies. Fingerprint identification involves analyzing the unique patterns on an individual's fingertip. Vein identification uses infrared light to scan the patterns of blood vessels in a person's hand or finger. Iris identification involves capturing and analyzing the patterns in the iris of a person's eye. Voiceprint identification analyzes the unique characteristics of a person's voice. Additionally, the market includes other identification technologies, such as facial recognition or palm print identification, which also use distinct biological features for identification purposes.</p></p>
