<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Plug-in Hybrid Drivetrain (PHE) market consists of three types: Tandem Series, Parallel Series, and Tandem-parallel Series. In Tandem Series, the gasoline engine powers a generator which charges the battery, and the electric motor directly powers the wheels. In Parallel Series, both the gasoline engine and electric motor provide power to the wheels simultaneously. Tandem-parallel Series combines elements of both Tandem Series and Parallel Series, allowing for flexible usage of the gasoline engine and electric motor. These different market types offer varying efficiencies and performance characteristics to cater to consumer preferences in the PHE market.</p></p>
