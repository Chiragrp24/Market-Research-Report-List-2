<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The solar thermal market consists of two main types: concentrated solar thermal (CST) and non-concentrated solar thermal (NCST). CST technology utilizes mirrors or lenses to concentrate sunlight onto a small area, generating heat that can be used for various applications such as electricity generation or heating fluids. NCST, on the other hand, uses flat-plate or evacuated tube collectors to directly absorb sunlight and convert it into heat. Both types have their advantages and applications, with CST often being used for large-scale projects and NCST for smaller-scale residential or commercial purposes.</p></p>
