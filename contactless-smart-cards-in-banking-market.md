<p><p>Contactless smart cards are a type of payment card used in banking that enable secure and convenient transactions without physical contact between the card and the reader. There are two main types of contactless smart cards: RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) and RFIC (Radio Frequency Integrated Circuit) cards. RFID cards rely on radio waves to transmit data between the card and the reader, while RFIC cards use integrated circuits for the same purpose. Both types offer fast and efficient transactions, making them popular in various banking sectors.</p></p>
