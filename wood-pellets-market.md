<p><p>The wood-pellet market can be classified into two types: white pellets and black pellets. White pellets are made from woody biomass and have a high calorific value. They are mainly used in residential heating and industrial applications. On the other hand, black pellets are produced from torrefied biomass and have a higher energy density. They are primarily utilized in power generation and as a coal substitute. Both types of pellets play a crucial role in renewable energy production and reducing carbon emissions in various sectors.</p></p>
