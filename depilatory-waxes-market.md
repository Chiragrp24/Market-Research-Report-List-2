<p><p>Depilatory waxes are available in two main types: frozen wax and hot wax. Frozen wax is a type of depilatory wax that needs to be chilled before use. It is convenient and mess-free, making the hair removal process quick and efficient. On the other hand, hot wax is heated before application. It has a lower melting point, which allows it to penetrate the hair follicles more deeply, resulting in smoother and longer-lasting results. Both types offer effective hair removal, but frozen wax is favored for its simplicity, while hot wax provides a more thorough treatment.</p></p>
