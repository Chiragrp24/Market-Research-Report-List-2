<p><p>The Wavemeter market consists of two main types: Michelson Interferometer-based and Fizeau Interferometer-based. Michelson Interferometer-based wavemeters measure optical frequencies by splitting a beam of light and analyzing the interference pattern formed. On the other hand, Fizeau Interferometer-based wavemeters determine the wavelength of light by analyzing the interference produced when a beam of light is reflected back from a reference mirror. Both types of wavemeters are commonly used in various industries for precise measurement of optical frequencies and wavelengths.</p></p>
